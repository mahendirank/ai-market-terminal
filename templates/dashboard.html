📋 RISK MANAGEMENT RULES

          
PROFESSIONAL TRADER GUIDELINES

        

      

      

        

          
✅ GOLDEN RULES

          

            • Never risk more than 1–2% per trade

            • Max daily loss: 3% of capital

            • Max drawdown: 10% — pause system

            • Always trade with 1:2+ R:R minimum

            • Size down after 2 consecutive losses
          

        

        

          
⚠ MAX LEVERAGE GUIDE

          

            • XAUUSD: safe ≤ 20x (max 500x)

            • NAS100/US30: safe ≤ 10x (max 100x)

            • BTCUSD: safe ≤ 5x (max 100x)

            • NIFTY F&O: safe ≤ 5x

            • BANKNIFTY F&O: safe ≤ 5x
          

        

        

          
🧮 POSITION SIZE FORMULA

          

            Risk Amount = Balance × Risk%

            Risk per Lot = SL points × Lot Value

            Lots = Risk ÷ Risk per Lot

            Leverage = Notional ÷ Balance
          

        

        

          
⛔ DANGER SIGNALS

          

            • Risk > 2% per trade → reduce size

            • R:R below 1:1.5 → skip trade

            • Leverage > safe limit → danger zone

            • 3 losses in a row → stop for day
